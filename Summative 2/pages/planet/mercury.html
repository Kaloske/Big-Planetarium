Mercury

Mercury, named after the Roman messenger god, is the smallest planet in our solar system. It is the closest planet to the sun, orbiting the sun every 88 Earth days.

Interestingly, because of the nature of its orbit, it is on average the closest planet to every other planet in the solar system on average over time.

Facts!

  • It is the Smallest Planet.

  • One year is 88 Earth Days.

  • The side facing the sun is around 430°C, with the side facing away being around -180°C.
