Earth

Our Home planet, currently the only planet known to possess biological life. Life is possible on our home plant thanks to the presence of water and oxygen.

Earth is fortunate enough to be in the "goldilocks" zone, neither too hot nor too cold for liquid water to exist. In fact, this is one characteristic that scientists look for when identifying planets that may potentially harbor life!

Facts!

  • Antarctica is technically a desert, as it is one of the driest places on Earth.

  • Earth is the only planet in the solar system with active plate tectonics.

  • Earth's orbit around the sun takes approximately 365.25 days. This why a leap year is needed every four years.
