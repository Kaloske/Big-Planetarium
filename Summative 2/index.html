The Milky Way Galaxy

Our planet Earth, home to billions of years of biological history is but a single planet in our Milky Way galaxy. Its name describes its appearance when viewed from earth, a milky like swirl.

Our galaxy consists of planets, stars, nebulas and gasses. It spans more than 100,000 light-years and contains 100-400 billion stars.
